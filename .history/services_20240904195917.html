BMS Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)

Ensure Optimal Performance with Our BMS AMC Services

A Building Management System (BMS) is crucial for the efficient operation of modern buildings. To keep your BMS running smoothly, it’s essential to have a reliable maintenance strategy in place. Our Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) offers comprehensive support to ensure your BMS operates at peak performance year-round.

Why Choose Our BMS AMC?

  • Proactive Maintenance:
    • Regular inspections and preventive maintenance to identify and address potential issues before they become major problems.
  • Expert Support:
    • Access to a team of highly skilled technicians with extensive experience in BMS systems.
  • 24/7 Emergency Response:
    • Round-the-clock support to address any unexpected issues promptly and minimize downtime.
  • Cost Efficiency:
    • Avoid costly repairs with our fixed annual fee structure, covering routine maintenance and service calls.
  • Comprehensive Coverage:
    • Includes calibration, software updates, system diagnostics, and component checks to ensure everything is functioning correctly.
  • Priority Service:
    • Priority scheduling for maintenance and emergency repairs, reducing wait times and ensuring swift resolution of any issues.
  • Detailed Reporting:
    • Regular reports on system performance, maintenance activities, and recommendations for improvements.
  • Customized Plans:
    • Tailored maintenance plans to suit the specific needs and size of your building, ensuring you get the most effective service.

Our AMC Services Include:

  • Routine Inspections: Regular checks to ensure all components are operating correctly and efficiently.
  • Preventive Maintenance: Scheduled maintenance tasks to prevent system failures and extend the life of your equipment.
  • Software Updates: Keeping your system software up-to-date with the latest features and security patches.
  • Calibration and Adjustment: Ensuring sensors and control systems are accurately calibrated for optimal performance.
